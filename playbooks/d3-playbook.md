핵심 학습 순서:

1. D3가 정말 필요한지 판단

3. axis 또는 legend-scale
4. join
5. tooltip
6. advanced interaction
7. cleanup / resize / mobile

- React: layout, state, controls
- D3: scale, axis, join, geometry, interaction
